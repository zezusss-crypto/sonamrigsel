The Gravitas | Case Study 01

Accelerating User Acquisition by 40% Through Market Repositioning

The Challenge (Problem)

The Gravitas faced saturation in its initial niche, leading to flat month-over-month user growth (less than 2%). The core product needed a new, scalable market segment to unlock future funding rounds.

My Solution (Action)

  1. Led a 3-month deep-dive analysis identifying the underserved regulatory compliance sector.
  2. Championed a product pivot, shifting resources to develop sector-specific features.
  3. Executed a refined marketing strategy focused on targeted industry events and thought leadership.

The Quantifiable Result (Result)

The strategic repositioning resulted in a **40% increase in qualified leads** within six months, unlocking a total addressable market 3x larger than the original projection.

EduLink | Case Study 02

Scaling User Onboarding to 15,000 Students in the First Year

The Challenge (Problem)

Launching a complex EdTech platform while ensuring low friction onboarding and high initial retention rates among diverse user segments (students, educators, institutions).

My Solution (Action)

  1. Implemented a tiered onboarding system utilizing product analytics to track drop-off points.
  2. Secured three major university partnerships through targeted B2B negotiation.
  3. Developed a scalable server architecture (AWS/Azure) to handle anticipated load without performance degradation.

The Quantifiable Result (Result)

Achieved a cumulative **15,000 active users** by the end of Year 1, with a churn rate 10% below industry average due to streamlined UX/UI.

Newton AI | Case Study 03

Monetizing Proprietary AI Models through a Flexible API System

The Challenge (Problem)

Newton AI had developed powerful internal ML models, but lacked a clear, scalable, and secure mechanism for third-party developers to integrate and pay for the service.

My Solution (Action)

  1. Defined the API architecture and documentation standards for external integration.
  2. Established tiered pricing models based on consumption (tokens/requests) and feature access.
  3. Oversaw the security audit and regulatory compliance for data transfer protocols.

The Quantifiable Result (Result)

Successfully launched the Newton API, resulting in **\$120,000 in recurring monthly revenue (MRR)** within the first nine months, validating the technical monetization strategy.
