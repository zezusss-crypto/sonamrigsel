Case Study 01

Accelerating User Acquisition by 40% Through Market Repositioning

The Challenge (Problem)

The Gravitas faced saturation in its initial niche, leading to flat month-over-month user growth (less than 2%). The core product needed a new, scalable market segment.

My Solution (Action)

  1. Led a 3-month deep-dive analysis into adjacent industries (e.g., FinTech).
  2. Championed a product pivot to service the underserved regulatory compliance sector.
  3. Re-allocated marketing spend towards highly targeted industry publications and events.

The Quantifiable Result (Result)

The repositioning resulted in a **40% increase in qualified leads** within six months, opening a total addressable market 3x larger than the original projection.
